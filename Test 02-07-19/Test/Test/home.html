The starting point for my idea was music. Music is a great interest of mine and sparks my creativity. Just as music has inspired me, I wanted to create a site that could give creative inspiration to others. The current working name is “9muses”, taken from the Goddesses of the Arts in Greek mythology. Muses or Mousai in Greek mythology are beautiful deities who inspired various arts and sciences. They were the inspiration for knowledge. It is believed that these muses were invoked by artists to seek blessings in their endeavours. The Nine Muses gave artists, philosophers and individuals the necessary inspiration for creation. Still today, the Muses are symbols of inspiration and artistic creation.
